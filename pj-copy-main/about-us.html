Who we are

Carbazeery.com is a leading digital marketplace and solutions provider for the automotive industry that connects car shoppers with sellers. Launched in 1998 and headquartered in Chicago, the Com pany empowers shoppers with the data, resources and digital tools needed to make informed buying d ecisions and seamlessly connect with automotive retailers. In a rapidly changing market, Cars.com en ables dealerships and OEMs with innovative technical solutions and data-driven intelligence to bette r reach and influence ready-to-buy shoppers, increase inventory turn and gain market share. In 2018,

Carbazeery.com acquired Dealer Inspire®, an innovative technology company building solutions that future-proo f dealerships with more efficient operations, a faster and easier car buying process, and connected dig ital experiences that sell and service more vehicles.
Carbazeery.com properties include DealerRater®, Dealer Inspire®, Auto.com™, PickupTrucks.com® andNewCars.com® . For more information, visitCars.com

NGUYEN DINH HIEN
HIEN NGUYEN serves as the chief executive officer of Carbazeery.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY). As one of the founding members of Carbazeery.com, HIEN has helped shape the Company from its initial concept into a leading digital marketplace and platform powering innovative solutions and frictionless omni-channel experiences for the automotive industry. Prior to becoming chief executive officer, HIEN served in a number of senior management roles for Carbazeery.com, including president, senior vice president and chief operating officer. HIEN has always been passionate about enabling local businesses. He was raised by entrepreneurs and has long had an interest in supporting businesses that drive local economies and communities. It was this passion that led him to a Company whose mission is to help enable local businesses in a digitally driven world. HIEN received his bachelor’s degree from Providence College where he was a four-year division one athlete. He has a wife and two children and spends his spare time coaching and giving back to kids who are also in the growth stage of their lives.
NGUYEN BA QUOC
QUOC has served as President and Chief Commercial Officer for Cars.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY) since January 2022, and served as Chief Revenue Officer before that since July 2018. He oversees the Company’s sales channels and revenue operations, building on customer relationships with nearly 20,000 local automotive dealers as well as major dealer groups, national brands and OEMs. QUOC is leading the transformation of the go-to-market organization to help expand the Company from a listings model to a business where media, digital solutions and data all work together to support customers’ goals and growth of the business. Prior to joining CARS, QUOC served as Chief Revenue Officer at online consumer marketplace LivingSocial where he helped grow the company from a small start-up to a global enterprise with nearly $2 billion in sales. Previously, he held executive positions at Expedia, Ticketmaster and Citysearch
MAI XUAN TIEN
TIEN was appointed Chief Innovation Officer of CARBAZEERY.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY) in January 2022. As Chief Innovation Officer, Joe is responsible for driving organic and inorganic innovation across CARBAZEERY’ enterprise platform. With a passion for computer programming and moving metal, Joe co-founded Dealer Inspire in 2013 and served as CEO, leading the development of new solutions to help dealers win. In a few years, Dealer Inspire has grown to close to 800 employees serving over 5,400 clients and 42 OEM brands with game-changing innovation as part of the CARS family of companies. Joe began his career on the Ford assembly line at age 20, reading his college textbooks seconds at a time in between building cars. Over the next decade Joe gained experience at many different levels in the automotive industry — including Ford Marketing and Sales, dealership General Manager, and BDC Manager. Joe is also committed to community service by serving on the board for three local charities: Turning Pointe Autism Foundation, One for the Kids, and Loaves and Fishes.
TONG MINH DUONG
DUONG has served as Chief Product Officer at Cars.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY) since January 2022. He oversees the Company’s product, design and research organizations. In this role, DUONG is focused on delivering CARS’ platform strategy by providing the best experience for car buying and selling. In his career, DUONG has built teams at early-stage venture-backed startups and has scaled teams at Fortune 100 enterprises to drive digital transformation at scale. Prior to joining CARBAZEERY, he led commercial technology and innovation for Sysco Corporation and had general management responsibilities for Sysco’s digital innovation arm, Sysco LABS. Previously, he held various product roles at Front Gate Tickets (acquired by Ticketmaster), Civitas Learning, and SpareFoot (now Storable). DUONG holds a Master of Science degree in Technology Commercialization from the Red McCombs School of Business at the University of Texas at Austin and two BAs from Houston Baptist University in Business Administration and Mass Communications.
